# **App Name**: iGrow Society

## Core Features:

- Futuristic Fintech Landing Page: A high-performance landing page mirroring the sleek, dark, and animated aesthetic of crypto.com to establish trust and authority in trading.
- AI Trading Mentor Tool: An interactive tool that uses reasoning to analyze a user's financial goals and suggests the most appropriate training program from the iGrow catalog.
- Program Showcase Tier: Interactive display cards for the Basic, Advanced, Advanced 2.0, Combo, and Internship programs including pricing and copy trading details.
- Member Benefit Portal: A dedicated dashboard showcasing value-added benefits like Live Market Learning and Free Signal Guides with an immersive UI.
- Secure Admission Workflow: A streamlined enrollment process for prospective students to apply for programs like the 50-month Combo Program.
- Live Market Sentiment Widget: A real-time visual indicator of market analysis trends, providing a futuristic look at professional trading education.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Electric Emerald (#00E676) for a vibrant, futuristic look that signals growth and prosperity.
- Background: Deep Void Green (#05100B), a heavily desaturated and dark base that provides a sleek, crypto-native backdrop.
- Accent: Polar Cyan (#B2EBF2) to provide a stark contrast for calls-to-action and secondary navigation elements.
- Headline and body font pairing: 'Space Grotesk' for its techy, geometric feel for headings, paired with 'Inter' for highly readable and objective body text.
- Glow-effect line icons representing trading concepts like rockets for growth, shields for security, and charts for progress.
- Layered depth design with frosted glass components and floating hero sections that respond to scroll movement.
- Subtle parallax scrolling effects and pulsating neon outlines on program cards to simulate a living fintech environment.
